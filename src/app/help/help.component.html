General

This tool is intended to visualize the data you get out of Google, Amazon, Facebook, Instagram, etc., if you request your GDPR data according to article 15.

If you do not know how to get your data and you live in Germany, you can use our tool, DSGVO-Generator, hosted at github.

How to use

Please download your data from the provider. Upload the CSV or JSON file (not the ZIP-file) and let it be displayed.

GDPR Notice

This tool does not transfer any data to a server. All your data is stored and processed locally in your browser.
